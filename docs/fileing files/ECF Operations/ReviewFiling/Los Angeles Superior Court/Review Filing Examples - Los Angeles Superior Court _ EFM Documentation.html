Impromptu Scenarios

Some EFSPs attempt to pass certification by simply re-using the sample scripts provided by JTI. To guard against such shortcuts, the last part of certification will require vendors to process 4-5 "impromptu" scenarios requested by JTI testers. These scenarios will demonstrate the overall technical strength of your implementation of our Core Concepts. For example, this could mean demonstrating:
  • how you would re-file the rejected document of a partially-accepted filing
  • adding multiple AKAs to a subsequent filing
  • processing more delayed NFRC scenarios such as post-judgement filings (writs, abstracts, etc)
  • how your application consumes our published XSD definitions
  • etc
